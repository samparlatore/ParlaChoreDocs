## 1. Executive Summary
ParlaChore is a **family-focused chore and relationship app** designed to make household management playful, collaborative, and emotionally resonant.  
It blends **scalable backend architecture** with **mascot-driven UX** to transform chores into shared experiences.

**Mission:** Help families build stronger routines and relationships through gamified chores, rewards, and playful themes.  
**Vision:** Become the go-to platform for family task management, combining technical rigor with joyful design.

---

## 2. Problem Statement
- Families struggle with **routine management**: chores, schedules, and accountability.
- Existing apps are either too **utilitarian** (cold task lists) or too **child-centric** (limited scope).
- Parents want **visibility and control**, while kids want **fun and engagement**.

---

## 3. Solution
ParlaChore offers:
- **Gamified chores** with avatars, rewards, and playful themes.
- **Role-based access** (Parent vs. Child) for accountability and safety.
- **Progressive enhancement UX**: accessible, crawlable, and emotionally resonant.
- **Secure account flow**: email verification, hashed credentials, planned MFA.
- **Customizable themes**: families choose mascots and backgrounds that reflect their personality.

## 5. Business Model
- **Freemium**: Core features free for 3 months (chore tracking, themes, basic rewards).
  - after 3 months, $1 per month per ParlaPal will be charged. Each account has a minimum of 2 ParlaPals.

    - Advanced themes and mascots.

    - Secure invite tokens and audit logging.
- **Partnerships**:
    - Branded themes with schools, community groups, or family brands.
- **Future Expansion**:
    - Integration with smart home devices (voice assistants, IoT chore reminders).
